Who am I?

Hi, I’m Adrian! A 17-year-old web development student at SintLucas. Originally from Poland and now living in the Netherlands, I’m passionate about creating engaging, user-friendly websites. Currently, I’m looking for an internship next year to apply my skills and grow my experience in web development.

My Background

My interest in tech started early. I took an ICT course in middle school. Currently, I have experience with HTML, CSS, and some JavaScript, along with skills in Figma for UX/UI design. Last year, I explored game development in Unity, which taught me a creative approach to coding.

What can I bring?

I’m creative, motivated, and quick to learn. I look forward to joining a team where I can contribute and grow, especially in front-end development and UX/UI design, while gaining insights into full-stack development.
